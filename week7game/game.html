Directions

Your mission description: To catch all the insects for your insect collection. You are a professional insect catcher with aspirations to catch at least one of all the orders of insects in the world.

Once you have read your cause and click start game, you will use the movement keys to wander the land of DOM in search of insects. Once you find an insect you would like to add to your collection, use the Ketchum button to catch the insect! You can view the insects that you have caught in your Insect Collection on the right!
